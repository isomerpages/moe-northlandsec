<p>Our school is proud to share that our Northstar, Loo Dan Lin, graduated
from ITE as one of the top graduates of 2024. Dan Lin was conferred the
Lee Kuan Yew (LKY) Gold Medal, at the ITE Graduation Ceremony. The LKY
Gold Medal is awarded to the top graduate of each ITE College, who has
excelled in his or her course of study and co-curricular activities, and
has made contributions to the community.</p>
<p>Dan Lin is currently pursuing a Diploma in Banking and Finance at Singapore
Polytechnic. Dan Lin is a true embodiment of a Learner who Shares, A Citizen
who Cares and A Leader who Dares. May you continue to shine in your future
endeavours, Dan Lin!</p>
