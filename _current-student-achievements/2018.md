<h4><strong>Zhang Tianyou (4R1) - Highest Mark In The World For Pearson Edexcel International GCSE Mathematics</strong></h4>
<p>Northland Secondary School is proud to announce that Zhang Tianyou (4R1) achieved the Highest Mark in the World Award for his Pearson Edexcel International GCSE. The International GCSE (IGCSE) is an equivalent qualification to the local &lsquo;O&rsquo; Levels, and Pearson Edexcel is one of three UK examination boards offering the IGCSE.</p>
<p>Tianyou has done us proud by obtaining the following Outstanding Learner Awards for his Pearson Edexcel IGCSE papers in January 2018:</p>
<ul>
<li>Highest Mark in&nbsp;<u>Singapore, Asia AND the World</u>&nbsp;for Mathematics</li>
<li>Highest Mark in Singapore for Physics</li>
</ul>
<p>The school would also like to thank his Mathematics Teacher, Mr Chen Weizhong, for his invaluable support for Tianyou, and for being such an inspiration for all his students.&nbsp;</p>

<h4><strong>Southeast Asian Pipe Band Championships 2018</strong></h4>
<p>On 9and 10 June 2018, the Pipe Band participated in the annual Southeast Asian Pipe Band Championships 2018. The competition is a platform for pipe bands in Southeast Asia to pit their skills against one another as well as promote Scottish bagpipe and drums&rsquo; music. Northland is proud to announce that Edmund Tan (2T3) and Kenneth Foo (3A4) achieved third and fourth positions for Solo in Snare Drum (Juvenile). In addition, Zhang Wenzheng (3A3) and Lua Chin Siang (2T3) achieved fourth place for Solo in Piping (Juvenile).</p>
