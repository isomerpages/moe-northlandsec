<h4><strong>Northstar shines!</strong></h4>
<p>The Media Computing Competition (MEC) is organised by Curriculum Planning and Development Division (CPDD), Sciences Branch, Computer Education Unit. The objective is to provide a platform to showcase students&rsquo; work; develop students&rsquo; interest in media design; and to hone students&rsquo; presentation and communication skills. Congratulations to Edwin Teo Yi Meng from 3A6 for being awarded the Platinum Award in the MEC Competition 2019 Game Category! Well done Northstar!&nbsp;</p>
